## XI. Case Management Workflow

### Phase Objectives

| Phase | Primary Objective | Key Actions |
|-------|------------------|-------------|
| Intake | Establish claim and representation | File C-4, gather employment records, document injury |
| Investigation | Verify compensability | Obtain C-3, medical records, witness statements |
| Treatment | Ensure proper medical care | Monitor ATP treatment, fight UR denials, track restrictions |
| MMI Evaluation | Determine permanent impairment | Obtain PPD rating, review IME, challenge if needed |
| Benefits Resolution | Maximize recovery | Negotiate PPD, vocational rehab, third-party claims |
| Settlement/Hearing | Resolve claim | Negotiate C&R, prepare for hearing, finalize benefits |

### Timeline Management
- **Day 1-3:** File C-4, notify insurer, document injury details
- **Week 1-2:** Confirm insurer received C-4, verify initial medical treatment
- **Day 30:** Follow up if no acceptance/denial from insurer
- **Monthly:** Review treatment progress, monitor benefit payments
- **At MMI:** Request PPD rating from ATP, review for adequacy
- **Post-MMI:** Evaluate settlement options, consider hearing if disputes remain

### Key Deadlines to Track
- 90-day C-4 filing deadline
- 30-day insurer response deadline
- Treatment authorization response deadlines
- Appeal deadlines for denials (varies by type)
- Statute of limitations for reopening claims
- PPD rating deadlines

### Case Status Categories
- **Active Treatment:** Employee receiving ongoing medical care
- **Modified Duty:** Employee working with restrictions
- **TTD:** Employee off work, receiving disability benefits
- **At MMI:** Treatment complete, awaiting PPD rating
- **PPD Dispute:** Challenging impairment rating
- **Settlement Negotiation:** Discussing C&R terms
- **Hearing Scheduled:** Contested issues pending hearing
- **Closed:** All benefits resolved and paid
