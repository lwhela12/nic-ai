## IV. Benefits Calculation

### Average Monthly Wage (AMW)
- Calculated from wages earned in the 12 weeks preceding the injury
- Include overtime, bonuses, and regular additional compensation
- Multiple employment: combine wages from all employers
- New employees: use agreed-upon wage rate or comparable employee wages
- Self-employed: based on reported income or industry standards

### TTD Benefits
- **Rate:** 66⅔% of AMW
- **State maximum:** Updated annually per NRS 616C.475
- **State minimum:** Updated annually
- **Waiting period:** First 5 days not compensated unless disability exceeds 20 days (then retroactive)
- **Payment schedule:** Every 15 days

### PPD Benefits
- Based on whole person impairment rating × applicable schedule
- **Schedule (NRS 616C.490):**
  - 0-5% WPI: Specific dollar amount per percentage point
  - 6-10% WPI: Higher rate per percentage point
  - 11-25% WPI: Increased rate
  - 26-50% WPI: Further increased rate
  - 51%+ WPI: Maximum rate per percentage point
- PPD benefits are in addition to any TTD previously received

### Vocational Rehabilitation Benefits
- Available when employee cannot return to pre-injury position
- Assessment by qualified vocational rehabilitation counselor
- Plan options: retraining, education, job placement assistance
- Maintenance benefits during rehabilitation period
- Maximum duration and benefit amounts per NRS 616C.590

### Supplemental Benefits
- Mileage reimbursement for medical travel
- Prescription drug coverage
- Prosthetic devices and durable medical equipment
- Home modification for severe disabilities
- Funeral expenses in death cases (NRS 616C.505)
