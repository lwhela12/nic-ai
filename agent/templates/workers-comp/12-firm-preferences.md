## XII. Firm-Specific Preferences

### Fee Structure
- Workers' compensation attorney fees regulated by Nevada DIR
- Fee schedule per NAC 616C regulations
- Costs advanced by firm where applicable
- Fee agreements must be approved by DIR

### Case Acceptance Criteria
- Clear work-related injury with documented mechanism
- Sufficient medical evidence supporting compensability
- Viable benefits or third-party claim potential
- Client cooperative and willing to follow medical treatment

### Communication Standards
- Return client calls within 24 hours
- Provide written status updates monthly
- Document all adjuster communications in writing
- Copy client on significant correspondence

### Settlement Evaluation
- Calculate total benefits owed vs. benefits paid
- Consider future medical needs and exposure
- Evaluate PPD rating adequacy before C&R
- Assess third-party claim potential before settling WC claim
- Ensure client understands C&R consequences (closing medical benefits)
